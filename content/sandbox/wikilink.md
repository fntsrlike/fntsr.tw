## Text Link Testing:

- normal md link: [My Note](note)
- single `[]`: [note]
- double `[]`: [[note]]
- double `[]` with alias: [[note|My Note]]
- double `[]` with number alias: [[note|100]]
- two link: [[Note1]] and [[Note2]]
